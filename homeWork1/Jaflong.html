Jaflong: First step out

Nov 25, 2018

It was a sunny day. We, a group of six people headed for Dhaka from Mymensingh at 4 am. It was dark. Out awated bus arived infront of campus gate. We jumped in without any work. It was a exciting moment.

When we arived at dhaka it was 7 am at morning. There was no time to waste. The Sylhet going train was going to departure at 9 am. The road was clear. We were lucky that we didn't face any heavy traffic. But the other group of three people form another campus had not arrived yet. It was almost 9 am. There is a bad reputation in Bangladesh railway for schedule disaster. So we expected that the train might be late 15 or 20 minute. It was our assumption so waited at station with a chill. when the other team arrived it was 9 am. Suddenly we heard a whistle. Oh no! the train started it's journey on time! We ran for the train. When we entered the platform. The train was gaining a slow pace. We ran for the train. It was like a movie. We jumped on the running train one by one.
